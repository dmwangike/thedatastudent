Client Profiling and Data Wrangling in SQL

Bodaboda is a word used in East Africa to mean motorbike taxi services. This is a convenient and ubiquitous mode of transport especially in areas where other forms of commute are not viable. Besides that, it has enabled many young people to have a source of income with the high unemployment rate. Most Bodaboda riders acquire the motorbikes through micro-credit and asset financing. The case study presented is based on a fictitious Bodaboda SACCO, which lends to its members through asset financing. This is derived from real data analysis test by an asset financing organization.

MovieLens Popular Genre-Pair

As part of our class work as Data Science students we were taken through the MovieLens Case Study, which is based on data collected on movie fans, details of movies and the rating of the respective movies. I took a tangent from the coursework, out of curiosity to try and find out, from the data provided, which genre pairs of the movies were most popular. Based on the assumption that people have a loyalty to certain genres, the insights from this review would help the Movie theatres determine which movies to screen.
