Food Hub Review

Foodhub is a food delivery service similar to Uber Eats. It uses an application to facilitate orders between customers and eaterie joints. For the service it charges a commission based on the cost of the orders. It has accumulated some data on customer behavior and the process from the time food is ordered to when it is delivered.This project is an analysis of the given data to enable better service delivery and to advise marketing initiatives on the same.
