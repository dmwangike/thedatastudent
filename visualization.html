VISUALIZING BANKING SELF-SERVICE ACTIVITIES

Jitihada Bank is a fictitious bank with various self-service channels enabling them to transact from anywhere within the country. This includes ATMs, Point of Sales Services, Cards that enable online payments and purchase at different merchants among others. This project is a visualization of the spread of the various services and the depth (count) of usage in the different regions. This is on an aggregating map that breaks down the activities as you zoom in on a region.
